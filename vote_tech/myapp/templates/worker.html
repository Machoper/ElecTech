Information for Poll Workers

Worker Information

There will be a number of voting booths and one registration booth.

Here is the procedure for executing the voting process.

1. Voters will line up to the registration booth and enter in their voting ID.

2. If a user is at registration booth for longer than one minute, there is a good chance that they are having trouble checking in. Please assist them by answering any questions if necessary.

3. Once they enter in their voting ID, they will be ready to move on to a voting booth.

4. Direct them to any open voting booth once they have checked in. If there are no voting booths available, have them wait until a voting booth is available.

5. When a user has finished voting, a physical record of their ballot will be automatically printed. Take this physical record and store it in a safe place for recount.

Users that have questions have been instructed to raise their hands for assistance. Be sure to assist them in a timely manner to reduce any bottlenecks in the system.
